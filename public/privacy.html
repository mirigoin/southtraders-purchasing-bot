Privacy Policy

South Traders Corp | Last updated: April 19, 2026

South Traders Corp ("we", "us", or "our") operates the SoutTradersBot WhatsApp application ("the Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information obtained through the Service, which is used exclusively for internal business-to-business communication with our authorized suppliers.

1. Information We Collect

The Service processes the following types of information when authorized suppliers communicate with us via WhatsApp:

  • Contact information: WhatsApp phone number and display name of the sender.
  • Message content: Text, images, and documents sent by suppliers relating to product quotations, stock availability, and commercial terms.
  • Message metadata: Timestamps, message identifiers, and delivery status.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Organize supplier quotations in an internal price comparison system.
  • Respond to supplier messages and coordinate purchasing decisions.
  • Maintain records of commercial negotiations for accounting and operational purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored in a secured PostgreSQL database hosted by Render, Inc. Access is restricted to authorized South Traders Corp personnel. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect data in transit (TLS encryption) and at rest.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Data may be shared only with service providers that support our operations (hosting, database, WhatsApp Business Platform) or legal authorities when required by applicable law.

5. Data Retention

We retain supplier communication data for the duration of the commercial relationship and up to 5 years thereafter for accounting, tax, and audit purposes, as required by applicable regulations.

6. Your Rights

Authorized suppliers may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal data by contacting us at the address below. See also our Data Deletion instructions.

7. Children Privacy

The Service is intended exclusively for business communication with commercial suppliers. It is not directed to children under 18, and we do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted at this URL with a revised last updated date.
