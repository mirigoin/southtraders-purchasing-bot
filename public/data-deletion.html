Data Deletion Instructions

South Traders Corp | Last updated: April 19, 2026

South Traders Corp respects your right to request the deletion of your personal data from our systems. This page explains how to submit a data deletion request and what to expect.

1. What Data We Store

When you, as an authorized supplier, communicate with our SoutTradersBot service via WhatsApp, we store:

  • Your WhatsApp phone number and display name.
  • The text content of messages you send relating to product quotations and commercial offers.
  • Images and documents you attach to those messages.
  • Timestamps and message metadata.

2. How to Request Data Deletion

To request the deletion of your personal data, please send an email to our data protection contact:

Email: info@southtraderscorp.com
Subject: Data Deletion Request
Include: Your WhatsApp phone number and a brief confirmation that you are the owner of that number.

3. What Happens Next

  • We will acknowledge your request within 5 business days.
  • We will verify your identity using the WhatsApp phone number you provide.
  • We will delete all personal data associated with your number within 30 days of verification.
  • We will send a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

4. Exceptions to Deletion

We may retain certain information when required by law (for example, for tax, accounting, or audit purposes). In such cases, the retained information will be anonymized where possible and stored securely with restricted access.

5. Additional Information

For more details on how we collect, use, and protect your data, see our Privacy Policy.
